Do You Recognize These Symptoms?

If conventional medicine hasn't found answers, your environment may be the cause.

🧠

Brain Fog & Fatigue

Formaldehyde, VOCs, and elevated CO2 impair cognitive function — often misdiagnosed as stress or aging.

🫁

Chronic Sinus Issues

Mold spores trigger inflammation, sinus infections, and asthma — antibiotics won't fix an environmental cause.

🔥

Autoimmune Flares

Mycotoxins from mold activate CIRS in ~25% of the population — your immune system stays stuck "on."

😴

Poor Sleep

Bedroom CO2 exceeding 1,200 ppm, allergens, and off-gassing furniture disrupt deep sleep cycles.

💧

Thyroid & Hormonal

PFAS "forever chemicals" in water disrupt thyroid function, liver health, and immune response.

🤢

Chemical Sensitivity

Total body burden exceeded — your body can't process one more chemical. Common after cumulative exposure.
